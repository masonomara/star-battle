# Star Battle Production Rules

## Overview

Star Battle is played on a square grid. The grid is divided into sperate regions. The goal is to place stars in the cells according to the rules.

## Rules

For a 1 star puzzle, you shoudl have exactly 1 star for each row, each column, and each region. You are not allowed to palce 2 stars in neighboring cells horizontally, verticlaly, or diagonally.

For 2 star puzzles, the rules are the same, but you have to have exactly 2 rows one ach row, column, and region.

For 3 star puzzles, they need to be 3, etc
