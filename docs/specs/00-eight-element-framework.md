## Users

**Primary: Star Puzzle Mastery Seekers**

- Puzzle sieve enables more difficult puzzles
- Other puzzles for experts don't have hints
- Difficulty calibration is sore point for users
- Will pay for challenges, not just removing ads
- Value hint quality over hint quantity
- Want to get better at a specific thing

**Secondary: Casual Star Battle Players**

- Popular versions already cater toward casual (NYT, LinkedIn)
- These games lean into daily habit hooks, social comparison
- Tolerate ads, expect free
- Won't pay for hints
- Lower lifetime value, higher volume
- Want something to do

**Design Approach**

- Focus on mastery seekers
- Convert casuals to mastery-seekers
- Casual-friendly intro pack (20 puzzles) with tutorial and free hints

## User Needs

**Core Needs**

- Challenge: not too easy, not impossible, tiered puzzles, flow state
- Completeness: sense of progression and collection
- Fairness/trust: puzzle respects my time, I know it's solvable
- Understanding: I know why this worked

**How We Deliver**

- Properly configured difficulty
- Tracking completed puzzles and packs
- Hints run the solver to show next cycle; template-based explanation mapped from rule name

## Business Goals

**Onboarding**

- Get mastery seekers to discover and download
- Convert curious casuals via intro pack

**Monetization**

- Primary: puzzle pack purchases (tiered by difficulty) - future feature
- Secondary: subscriptions for ad-free experience, unlimited hints, unlocking all puzzles (separate and combo options)
- Tertiary: passive banner ads (omnipresent, not rewarded)

**Retention**

- Daily/weekly/monthly challenges to build habit
- Progression systems
- New pack releases

## Investments

- Puzzles completed, packs finished, progression
- Stats on solve times for any puzzle complete
- Daily, weekly, monthly streaks
- Purchases (subscription status, packs owned)

## Rewards

**Intrinsic**

- Mastery validation
- For mastery seekers, challenge is the reward
- Don't bury hard content behind grind — let skill unlock it

**Progression**

- Unlocking harder difficulty tiers
- Pack completion status
- More puzzles

**Streaks**

- No tangible reward—streak display IS the reward (loss aversion)

## Desired Actions

**Engagement**

- Complete puzzles
- Return daily for challenges

**Monetization**

- Purchase puzzle packs
- Subscribe (ad-free, hints, unlocks)

**Retention**

- Maintain streaks
- Complete packs
- Progress to harder difficulties

**Growth**

- Rate/review the app (prompt on pack completion)
